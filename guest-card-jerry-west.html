Jerry West

A Life Beyond Basketball

The NBA logo — one of basketball's greatest legends — opens up about what statistics could never capture.

"I really didn't know what love was as a kid... I think there's so many chips in the granite that they never really heal."

— Jerry West, age 82

At 82 years old, Jerry West shares what he's rarely spoken about: losing his brother in Korea when he was 13, surviving an abusive father, the depression he still battles, and the grief of losing Kobe.
